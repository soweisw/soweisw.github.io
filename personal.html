Family Digitization Videos

About a year or so ago, I found boxes of physical media in my grandmother's basement, including CDs, VHS tapes, film reels, and even 35mm slides as old as 1913. After digitizing all ~2,000 media files, I sorted, reviewed, and edited the best footage into 5 themed videos, each 30-45 minutes long and representing a different section of time. After all this, I held a viewing party for my grandmother's 80th birthday, and it was absolutely lovely. The videos are unlisted, and, for privacy reasons, I'm only including the video chronicling the oldest time frame.
